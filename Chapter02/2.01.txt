the differences between int, long, long long and short:
	int, and short have a minimum size of 16 bits or two bytes
	long has a minimum size of 32 bits or four bytes
	long long has a minimum size of 64 bits or eight bytes

the differences between an unsigned and a signed type:
	an unsigned type represents values that are greater than or equal to zero
	a signed type represents negative or positive numbers

the differences between a float and a double:
	a float has a minimum size of 6 significant digits
	a double has a minimum size of 10 significant digits
